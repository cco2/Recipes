Irish Coffee
======
Irish coffee

## Ingredients
* 1oz irish whiskey
* 0.5oz demerol syrup
* 3.5oz hot coffee
* 0.5oz mr.black coffee liqueur (optional)
* fresh whipped cream

## Tools
* Irish Glass Coffee Mugs (or any cup)
* jigger

## Steps
1. pour whiskey, syrup, coffee and coffee liqueur (optional) into coffee mug
2. top off with fresh whipped cream
